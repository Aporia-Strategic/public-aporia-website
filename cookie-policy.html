Cookie Policy

Last Updated: February 2026

1. Our Approach to Cookies

APORIA STRATEGIC (Pty) Ltd believes in a clean, privacy-first web experience. Because this website serves as a simple informational landing page, we do not use analytical, tracking, advertising, or third-party cookies (such as Google Analytics or Facebook Pixel).

2. What the Site Uses

This website is hosted on the Netlify platform. Any cookies or session storage placed on your browser by our site are strictly limited to the "Essential" category—meaning they are technically required for the website to load securely and function correctly on your device.

3. Managing Cookie Preferences

Because we do not deploy non-essential cookies, we do not require a cookie consent banner. However, you can always control or disable all cookies through your web browser's settings.

4. Changes to this Policy

If we ever introduce complex functionality or tools directly to this landing page that require additional cookies, we will update this policy accordingly.
